About Tekflix

Welcome to Tekflix

Tekflix is a cinema app designed for Tekup University students, offering a wide selection of movies and exclusive content. Watch movies, discover new releases, and enjoy a cinematic experience tailored just for you.

Explore different genres, watch trailers, and dive deep into movie details all from one place. Whether you're a film lover or just looking for a quick movie night, Tekflix has something for everyone!
